Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Candy Goal ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Candy Goal is a casual puzzle game. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the App.

The App may collect the following types of information automatically:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for advertising purposes.
  • Usage Data: General app usage statistics such as session duration and interactions within the game.
  • Advertising Data: Our advertising partners may collect data to serve relevant ads (see Section 3).

2. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the game experience
  • Display advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Save your game progress locally on your device

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Data Storage

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device using the device's built-in storage. We do not store your personal data on any external servers.

5. Children's Privacy

Candy Goal is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Ad Personalization

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
